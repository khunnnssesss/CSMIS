About Our Cathering Service
Started from 2020

CSMIS LUNCH BOX was founded by DirAceTechnology for best experience of employees'lunch. LUNCHBOX is a food organization that offers different categories of food to fit the better lunch of every employee. We know it is hard for others to make up their minds on what they want so why not offer a little of everything. And each employee can choose what they want to eat, what meat they want to avoid and complain about the taste of their lunch to the resturant that we offer. They can also reply feedbacks to our admins and also comment in our website page. We offer excellent service with the best experience of our company. And our company' heads are always thinking about their employees to give opportunity for every employee & better allowances. Our Menu is especially in Burmese Cuisine and sometime for Chinese Cuisine.We only use hygienic ingredients.

That is overall details about of our service.
